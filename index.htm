IIDR was established in August 2000 with the following aims and objectives:

1 To disseminate Islamic knowledge and worldview
2 To research on areas of contemporary relevance
3 To promote a balanced, moderate and comprehensive interpretation of Islam
4 To bridge the gap between youth and scholars, knowledge and activism, traditionalism and modernity, Muslims and the wider society
5 To promote change, progress, development and creativity
